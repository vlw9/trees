Topic

I want to create a site where writers and artists can engage. Writers can upload short (or long) stories to the site and their fellow writers can browse the works and leave comments/thoughts/suggestions. Readers also have the opportunity to keep a working list of their favorite stories so they can return to them at a later date. On the flip side, artists (who also have access to the writing tools) do the same thing; they upload their works to an audience of primarily other artists and writers, whereupon they can receive feedback from their peers. They can also "pin" their favorite pieces, or simply pieces that they would like to observe again at a later date.

 

This idea interests me because I am an active member in both the writing and the painting circles, and we currently only post on separate Facebook groups. It would be very cool to have our own site to work with; even cooler if my two societies could unite and learn from each other.
